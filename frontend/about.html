About Krishi AI

Krishi AI is an intelligent assistant designed for farmers. It predicts crops based on soil and weather conditions. Features include voice input, TTS, real-time recommendations, and more in the future like disease detection and multilingual support.

🌱 Our Mission

To revolutionize Indian agriculture through technology, making smart farming accessible to every kisan — from villages to metros.

🔍 Why Krishi AI?

Because most Indian farmers don’t have access to real-time data, scientific crop advice, or digital tools. Krishi AI bridges that gap using AI-based recommendations and user-friendly design — even for non-tech-savvy farmers.
